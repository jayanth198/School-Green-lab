WORKFLOW ACTIONS:
1. Submission Action: Notify Teacher via Email/Push when Class > 0.
2. Image Storage: Connected to Cloudinary or Google Drive (Standard for Glide/AppSheet).
3. Offline Logic: Enabled in Builder settings (Offline data storage on local cache).
4. Reporting: Pivot Table in Google Sheets for "Count of Observations per Class per Month".

PERMISSIONS REQUIRED:
- camera: To capture site photos.
- microphone: To allow students to record thoughts (Voice-to-Text).
- storage: To cache offline submissions.
